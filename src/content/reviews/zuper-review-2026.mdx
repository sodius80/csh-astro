## What Zuper Gets Right

<p><strong>Intelligent scheduling automation</strong> -- Zuper’s core strength appears to be scheduling and dispatch automation across jobs, technicians, locations, and availability. For roofing and other field service contractors juggling different job types, territories, and crew availability, this could reduce the manual scheduling burden that hurts productivity in growing operations.</p>

<p><strong>Project-based workflow design</strong> -- Zuper’s platform can support project-style field service workflows instead of only simple one-off service calls. This aligns better with how roofing jobs actually work, where a single project involves multiple visits, material deliveries, and coordination between office and field teams.</p>

<p><strong>Customer visibility features</strong> -- Zuper lists customer portal, payments, and integration products across its platform, but buyers should confirm which portal and project-management capabilities are included in their quote. For an industry where communication gaps often create friction, this transparency can help contractors stand out and reduce phone calls about job status.</p>

## Where Zuper Falls Short

<p><strong>Pricing confusion creates evaluation barriers</strong> -- Current software-directory listings generally show pricing available on request, while unofficial estimates conflict. Contractors need to know what they're buying before committing time to demos and setup.</p>

<p><strong>Limited verified user feedback</strong> -- Unlike established platforms with thousands of reviews, Zuper lacks the user feedback volume that helps contractors understand real-world performance. This makes it harder to assess reliability during busy seasons or complex projects.</p>

<p><strong>Feature complexity may overwhelm smaller teams</strong> -- The all-in-one approach with AI scheduling, project coordination, and warehouse inventory tracking could be overkill for contractors who just need basic job management and aren't ready for advanced automation.</p>
