<h2>Feature Deep Dive</h2>

<h3>Email Campaign Builder</h3>
<p>Moosend's drag-and-drop email editor is clean, modern, and intuitive. Non-technical users can build professional campaigns from 130+ pre-designed templates without touching code. The editor supports smart content blocks that adapt per subscriber, conditional visibility (show different content to different segments within the same email), and image manipulation tools. An AI Writer generates subject lines and body copy suggestions, which is useful for contractors who struggle with what to say in a newsletter.</p>

<p>The catch: the automation message builder is a separate editor from the main campaign builder, and some users report inconsistencies between the two. If you build an automation email, the editing experience differs slightly from a standard campaign email. It is not a deal-breaker, but it adds a learning curve.</p>

<h3>Marketing Automation</h3>
<p>The automation builder is a visual workflow editor that handles the scenarios contractors care about: welcome sequences for new subscribers, time-delayed follow-ups after a quote request, abandoned form recovery, and behavior-triggered campaigns based on opens, clicks, or page visits.</p>

<p>Every Pro plan includes unlimited automation workflows. There is no tier where you lose automation features. That is the single biggest advantage Moosend has over competitors that gate automation behind higher plans. A contractor paying $7/mo gets the same automation capability as one paying $70/mo.</p>

<p>Where it falls short: the automation logic is simpler than ActiveCampaign's. Moosend handles conditional triggers and delays well, but if you need complex multi-branch split paths with scoring thresholds, site tracking event chains, or pipeline-stage-based automation, ActiveCampaign is the stronger tool.</p>

<h3>Landing Pages and Forms</h3>
<p>Moosend includes a landing page builder and subscription form builder on every Pro plan. The landing page templates are built to convert and include analytics. For a contractor who wants a simple "request a quote" landing page or a seasonal promotion page without paying for a separate tool like Leadpages, this is real value.</p>

<p>The subscription forms capture customer preferences and enable targeted messaging. You can embed forms on your website or use Moosend's hosted pages. The free trial limits you to 1 unpublished landing page and 1 unpublished subscription form, so you cannot publish live forms until you upgrade to Pro.</p>

<h3>AI Writer and Predictive Analytics</h3>
<p>Moosend includes an AI Writer that generates subject lines and email copy from prompts. It also offers AI-powered product recommendations for e-commerce users and audience discovery tools that predict location, demographics, and weather-based analytics. For a contractor sending a seasonal promotion, the AI Writer can draft a subject line and opening paragraph, which you then edit and send.</p>

<p>The predictive analytics go beyond basic email metrics. Moosend tracks product views, carts, and purchases (for e-commerce integrations), plus custom events you define. Real-time open and click-through rates, bounces, unsubscribes, and spam complaints are standard across all plans.</p>

<h3>Reporting and Analytics</h3>
<p>All plans include real-time reporting, Google Analytics tracking, email heatmaps, geography and device reporting, spam analysis, and A/B testing. Custom reports with multiple widgets are available on Pro and Enterprise (add-on on Moosend+).</p>

<p>The catch: multiple Capterra reviewers note that the reporting interface is not intuitive. Generating custom reports requires navigating through menus that are not immediately obvious. If reporting is your primary buying criterion, test this during the 30-day trial before committing.</p>

<h2>What Moosend Gets Right</h2>

<h3>One plan, all features</h3>
<p>The single smartest decision Moosend made was putting every Pro feature on one plan. Automation, A/B testing, landing pages, segmentation, real-time analytics, AI Writer, SMTP server - all included at $7/mo for 500 contacts. Compare that to MailerLite, which gates advanced automation behind the Growing Business plan ($10/mo for 500), or ActiveCampaign, which requires the Plus plan ($30/mo for 500) for automation features. A contractor who wants professional email marketing without feature anxiety gets it here.</p>

<h3>The lowest real entry price</h3>
<p>$7/mo annual for 500 contacts is the floor. Moosend does not have a free forever tier, but the 30-day trial with no credit card delivers full feature access for up to 1,000 contacts. That is enough time and capacity for a contractor to build a contact list, design a newsletter template, set up an automation workflow, and send real campaigns before paying anything.</p>

<h3>Fast, accessible support</h3>
<p>Moosend's live chat support is frequently cited in Capterra reviews as a standout. Response times of 5-30 minutes are common, even on lower-tier plans. The support team is available 24/5 via email and live chat. For a contractor who is not technical and needs help setting up their first automation or troubleshooting a deliverability issue, this matters more than feature count.</p>

<h3>Pay-as-you-go for seasonal senders</h3>
<p>Not every contractor sends emails year-round. Some HVAC companies send heavy promotions in spring and fall, then go quiet. Moosend's email credits system (350,000 emails for $350, scaling up to 1,000,000 for $1,000) lets seasonal senders pay for sends without a monthly subscription. Credits never expire, and each credit equals one email to one recipient. This is a unique advantage over platforms that require monthly commitments.</p>

<h2>Where Moosend Falls Short</h2>

<h3>No mobile app</h3>
<p>This is the most common complaint across Capterra reviews. Moosend does not have a dedicated iOS or Android app. The web platform can be accessed from a mobile browser, but multiple reviewers describe it as "not ideally mobile-friendly." For a contractor who wants to check campaign performance or send a quick broadcast from the field, this is a real limitation. MailerLite, ActiveCampaign, and GetResponse all have mobile apps.</p>

<h3>No CRM or pipeline tracking</h3>
<p>Moosend is an email marketing platform, not a sales CRM. It has no deal stages, pipeline views, or contact scoring beyond email engagement metrics. If your problem is lead tracking and follow-up discipline before the quote goes out, you need a tool like <a href="/pipedrive-review/">Pipedrive</a> or <a href="/pipeline-crm-review/">Pipeline CRM</a>. Moosend handles the marketing side; it does not manage the sales pipeline.</p>

<h3>Smaller integration ecosystem</h3>
<p>Moosend lists 90+ integrations. ActiveCampaign has 850+. MailerLite has 140+. The integrations that matter for contractors are covered (WordPress, WooCommerce, Zapier, Salesforce, Shopify), but if you rely on niche tools or need deep native sync with accounting software, the library is thinner than the larger platforms.</p>

<h3>Pricing scales steeply at higher contact counts</h3>
<p>The $7/mo entry price is excellent for 500 contacts. But Moosend pricing scales by contact count across 12 tiers. At 10,000 contacts, the annual price is approximately $70/mo. At 50,000 contacts, you are in custom-quote territory. For contractors whose lists grow through customer acquisition, the cost advantage shrinks as the audience grows.</p>

<p>ActiveCampaign has deeper automation logic, a built-in CRM, more integrations, and a mobile app. Moosend is half the price ($7 vs $15 for 500 contacts) and includes all features on one plan. If you need <a href="/getresponse-vs-moosend/">marketing automation</a> with multi-branch logic and CRM features, ActiveCampaign wins. If you need affordable email marketing with solid automation and no feature tiers, Moosend delivers more value per dollar.</p>

<h3>Moosend vs MailerLite</h3>
<p>MailerLite has a free forever tier (1,000 contacts, 12,000 emails/mo) and a mobile app. Moosend does not have a free tier or mobile app. On pricing, Moosend's $7/mo annual for 500 contacts is cheaper than MailerLite's $10.80/mo for the same count.
