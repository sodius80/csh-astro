**Short verdict:** Estimate Rocket is a solid all-in-one platform for service contractors in 50+ trades who want estimating, CRM, scheduling, project management, and invoicing in a single system - without paying extra for core features. It is not the right fit for contractors who need offline mobile access or heavy construction project management.

## Who Should Buy Estimate Rocket

Estimate Rocket fits service contractors whose workflow spans the full lead-to-paid lifecycle and who want a single platform rather than stitching together separate tools. The strongest candidates are:

- **Painting, remodeling, and general contractors** who produce detailed estimates with trade-specific line items and need proposals, scheduling, and invoicing connected to those estimates
- **HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contractors** who want to manage leads, schedule service calls, and invoice from the same system
- **Roofing, landscaping, and home improvement contractors** who need professional estimates with photos, terms, and e-signatures that go out fast
- **Growing businesses with 3-15 users** who want all features available without upgrading to access basic functionality

## Who Should Skip Estimate Rocket

Estimate Rocket is a poor fit for:

- **Contractors who need offline mobile access** - the app requires an internet connection (offline mode is in development)
- **Heavy construction and commercial contractors** whose estimating and project management needs exceed the residential and light commercial scope
- **Solo contractors on a tight budget** - <a href="/jobber-review/">Jobber</a> at $29/mo or <a href="/best-estimating-software-for-small-contractors/">Joist</a> at $10/mo are lighter and cheaper for simple estimates and invoicing
- **Contractors who need digital takeoff tools** - Estimate Rocket does not include plan-based takeoff capabilities

## Pricing Breakdown

Estimate Rocket offers three plans with a 30-day free trial on all tiers. Every plan includes all core features - there is no gating of estimating, CRM, scheduling, or invoicing behind higher tiers.

| Plan | Monthly | Full Users | Additional Full User | Field User |

| Launch | $139 | 3 | $79 | $10 |
| Accelerate | $259 | 7 | $39 | $10 |

**What is included on every plan:** All core features - CRM, estimating with trade-specific templates, proposals with e-signatures, scheduling, project management, invoicing, client portal, automated follow-ups, time tracking, 25+ reports, QuickBooks Online integration, Zapier connectivity, unlimited clients and projects, chat and phone support.

**Free onboarding:** Every plan includes professional startup services (normally valued at $450): a 45-minute live personalized training session for your entire organization, terms and conditions customization, basic Zapier configuration, data cleanup and import prep, and template setup.

**The real cost:** Launch at $139 per month for 3 users is the most affordable way to get the full feature set. The jump to Accelerate at $259 per month adds 4 more full user seats and lowers the per-user cost significantly. At $19 per additional user on Expand, the per-seat cost is competitive with any platform in this category.

**Free trial:** 30 days with full access and no long-term commitment.

**Money-back guarantee:** 90 days - cancel and receive a full refund on any payments made in the first three months.

## What Estimate Rocket Does Well

### All Core Features on Every Plan

The most unusual thing about Estimate Rocket's pricing is that every plan includes the full feature set. Most contractor software platforms in this range gate key functionality - CRM on higher tiers, advanced reporting on the top plan, scheduling only if you pay more. Estimate Rocket does not do that. The $139 Launch plan gives you the same CRM, estimating tools, scheduling, invoicing, client portal, and reporting that the $359 Expand plan does. The difference is only the number of user seats.

This matters for a growing business. You are not forced into a bigger plan to unlock a feature you need. Add users as you hire, not as a penalty for needing project management.

### Trade-Specific Estimate Templates

Estimate Rocket includes 57+ trade-specific estimate templates covering 50+ trades. A painting contractor gets pricing and line items tuned to painting. A roofer gets roofing-specific estimate structures. A landscaper gets landscaping templates. The templates are built around real industry workflows rather than generic line item forms.

For contractors who work in multiple trades - a common situation in home improvement - this is genuinely useful. One platform handles all the trade types you serve with consistent proposal formats and pricing structures.

### Free Onboarding That Actually Saves Setup Time

The free onboarding package (normally $450) includes a 45-minute live training session, terms and conditions customization, a basic Zapier connection, data cleanup and import, and template setup. Most contractor software charges extra for onboarding or leaves you to figure it out from documentation. Estimate Rocket's approach is practical: a real person walks your team through setup, and you are producing estimates within a few hours rather than a few days.

### Generous Risk Reversal

The combination of a 30-day free trial and a 90-day money-back guarantee is unusually generous. Most contractor platforms offer one or the other. Estimate Rocket offers both: you get a full month to evaluate with no commitment, and if you continue, you still have 90 days to change your mind and get every dollar back. That is a confident sign from the company about their retention rates.

### Strong Customer Support

Across user reviews, customer support is the most consistently praised aspect of Estimate Rocket. Multiple reviewers describe the support team as responsive, helpful, and easy to reach by phone, chat, or email. For a tool that serves contractors who are not always tech-comfortable, this matters. A platform is only as good as the help you get when something goes wrong.

## Where Estimate Rocket Falls Short

### No Offline Mobile Access

Estimate Rocket is a web-based application. It works on mobile browsers, and the interface is genuinely usable on phones and tablets. But it requires an internet connection. Crews in basements, rural job sites, or areas with poor cell coverage cannot use the app. Offline access is listed as in development, but as of mid-2026 it is not available. For contractors whose field teams regularly work without reliable connectivity, this is a meaningful gap.

### Residential and Light Commercial Scope

Estimate Rocket is built for service contractors and light commercial work. It is not designed for heavy construction, large commercial projects, or complex construction management workflows that require RFIs, submittals, change orders, or lien waivers. If your projects are measured in millions of dollars and span multiple prime contractors, this is not the platform for you.

### Advanced Training Is Separate

The free onboarding session covers getting started - templates, data import, basic configuration. But contractors who want to go deeper (custom reporting workflows, advanced automation, multi-location setup) need the separate advanced user training, which is not included in the standard package. The pricing page does not list the cost of advanced training, so that is a question to ask before committing.

### Integrations Are Mostly Zapier-Mediated

QuickBooks Online has a native integration. For everything else - Google Workspace, email marketing, CRMs, project management tools - connectivity goes through Zapier. Zapier is well-established and reliable, but it adds a layer of configuration and potential failure point that native integrations do not. Contractors who want direct connections to the rest of their software stack should factor in Zapier setup time.

### No Digital Takeoff Tools

Estimate Rocket does not include digital plan takeoff capabilities. Contractors who need to measure quantities from blueprints, PDF plans, or digital drawings will need separate takeoff software or a product like <a href="/buildxact-review/">Buildxact</a> that combines takeoffs with estimating.

## Alternatives

### Jobber

Jobber starts at $29 per month on annual billing and is the strongest alternative for solo contractors and small teams who need basic scheduling, quoting, and invoicing connected to service workflow. It does not include the depth of estimating tools or trade-specific templates that Estimate Rocket offers. Best for contractors whose estimating needs are simple quotes rather than detailed line-item estimates.

### Housecall Pro

<p>Housecall Pro starts at $49 per month and is a strong option for field-service contractors (HVAC, plumbing, electrical) who want a mobile-first platform optimized for dispatches and service calls. It includes scheduling, dispatch, invoicing, and payment processing. The estimating capabilities are lighter than Estimate Rocket's. Best for contractors whose primary workflow is dispatching technicians rather than producing detailed estimates. See our <a href="/housecall-pro-review/">full Housecall Pro review</a>.</p>

### Joist

Joist at $10 per month for Basics is the lowest-cost option in this space. It covers estimates, invoices, payments, e-signatures, and basic client tracking. No cost database, no trade-specific templates, no CRM depth. Best for solo contractors who send simple documents and do not need the full operations platform.

### Clear Estimates

Clear Estimates at $79 per month is the best option for contractors who want a maintained cost database with 13,000+ line items and preloaded project templates. It does not include CRM, scheduling, or invoicing. Best for residential remodelers who need a deep cost library rather than an end-to-end platform.

### Buildxact

Buildxact starts at $199 per month and adds digital takeoffs, job management, purchase orders, and scheduling alongside estimating. It is the best option for contractors who need estimates to feed into managed job tracking and plan-based takeoffs. The tradeoff is more complexity and a higher entry price.

## Bottom Line

Estimate Rocket is a solid all-in-one platform for the service contractor who wants one system from lead to payment. The all-core-features-on-every-plan pricing model is genuinely different from most competitors, and the combination of a 30-day free trial with a 90-day money-back guarantee makes it unusually low-risk to evaluate.

It is the wrong tool for three types of buyers: contractors who need offline mobile access, heavy construction contractors whose needs go beyond the residential and light commercial scope, and solo contractors on a tight budget who can get by with cheaper alternatives.

The 30-day free trial is the best way to evaluate. Contractors who sign up should take advantage of the free onboarding session - the 45-minute live training with a real person who helps set up templates and data import is worth significantly more than the included cost suggests.
