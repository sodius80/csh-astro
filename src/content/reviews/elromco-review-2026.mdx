## Feature Deep Dive

### Instant Online Quotes - The Signature Differentiator

The feature that sets Elromco apart from most moving software is the instant online quote. You embed a white-labeled form on your website that calculates a real binding price instantly based on your rate tables, fuel surcharges, seasonal pricing tiers, and the customer's inventory. The customer sees a dollar price - not a "we'll get back to you" message.

This matters because the moving industry standard is a lead form followed by a phone call or email with a price. That callback delay is where leads go cold. Elromco reports that moving companies using instant online quotes see booking rates climb from roughly 15% to over 30% - a direct result of showing a real price while the customer is actively comparing movers.

The quoting engine supports three pricing models (hourly, flat rate, long distance), six seasonal rate tiers, 12 fuel surcharge strategies, and 16 configurable fee types. Long-distance and interstate moves use mileage tariffs, FMCSA compliance data, SIT (storage-in-transit), and weight ticket integration.

### Interactive Client Portal

The client portal is white-labeled on your domain - customers never see Elromco branding. Inside the portal, customers can update their inventory (add or remove items), change move dates, adjust packing service levels, and the quote recalculates in real time. This reduces the back-and-forth calls that normally happen when a customer's move details change between booking and moving day.

For moving companies that handle corporate relocations or affiliate partner moves, Elromco includes dedicated B2B portals with lifetime revenue tracking and multi-order management - a feature most moving software platforms do not offer.

### Online Booking with E-Sign and Deposit

Elromco's booking flow is not a lead form. The office opens available time slots, the customer selects one, completes e-signature, pays a deposit (if required), and the job locks into dispatch - all without the office touching it. The system runs 24/7, so customers can book outside business hours.

### Real-Time 9-Stage Job Tracker

Once a move is underway, Elromco tracks it through nine stages: confirmed, pre-move survey completed, crew assigned, truck loaded, en route, arrived, unloading, delivered, and completed. Each stage carries an exact timestamp and a comparison of estimated vs. actual time and cost. The tracker is visible to both the office and the customer through the client portal.

For operations managers, this replaces the "where is the truck" phone calls with a real-time dashboard. Elromco customer reviews report 40% fewer disputes after adopting the tracker, because both the customer and the office have a timestamped record of every event.

### Electronic BOL with Auto-Payroll

When the foreman submits the electronic bill of lading from the crew portal, the system closes the job, calculates payroll (hourly rates, commissions, per-job bonuses, tip splitting - all branch-specific), and generates the invoice. The data flows into accounting without anyone re-typing numbers.

This is the operational win that separates a purpose-built moving platform from a general CRM connected to a separate payroll tool. In a generalist setup, someone has to match timesheets to jobs, calculate commission, and create invoices separately. In Elromco, it happens when the foreman hits submit.

### Elromco AI

AI is built into every text field across the platform - not as a separate feature but as a layer available wherever you type. Tone adjustment (formal to friendly and back), instant translation, auto-reply suggestions, and speech-to-text are available in the communication hub, CRM notes, and customer messages.

The AI inventory feature lets crews snap a photo of a room and auto-detect items, quantities, cubic footage, weight, and room labels. This speeds up the inventory survey process significantly compared to manual item-by-item entry.

### Multi-Branch Management (Enterprise)

The Enterprise plan ($399/mo) includes a unified multi-branch dashboard. All locations appear on one screen with per-branch reporting, rate tables, crews, and dispatch settings. The owner or operations manager can filter by branch, compare performance, and move resources between locations - all from one login.

The single most practical advantage Elromco offers is what it does not make you do: book a demo to find out the price. Professional at $289/mo and Enterprise at $399/mo are on the website. No "schedule a call to see pricing," no request-a-quote form, no sales-led price discovery. This gives a moving company owner a real budget number before investing time in a demo.

In a category where most competitors (<a href="/best-moving-software/">SmartMoving, Supermove, MoveitPro</a>) hide pricing behind a sales call, this is a meaningful structural advantage for buyers.

### Moving-Specific Workflow Across the Entire Job Lifecycle

Elromco is not a general CRM with moving templates. The pricing engine understands seasonal rates and fuel surcharges. The dispatch board is built for trucks and crews, not generic resources. The eBOL is a moving-specific legal document, not a generic work order. The inventory module knows cubic footage and weight. Every feature reflects how moving companies actually operate.

### Electronic BOL-to-Payroll Automation

The direct pipeline from eBOL submission to payroll calculation is the feature that saves the most back-office time. For a moving company running 50-200 jobs per month, eliminating the manual step between "job completed" and "paycheck calculated" saves hours per week and removes a common source of payroll errors. Accounting teams consistently cite this as a top reason for staying on the platform.

## Where Elromco Falls Short

### Professional Plan Caps Office Users at 3

The Professional plan at $289/mo includes 3 office users. Crew members are unlimited, but if your office team - dispatchers, sales reps, accounting - has more than 3 people, you need Enterprise at $399/mo. This is a straightforward price jump that growing companies should factor in from the start.

### No Self-Serve Free Trial

Elromco requires booking a demo to evaluate the platform. There is no self-serve free trial where you can create an account and test the features hands-on. Most moving companies report being live within two weeks of the first call, and onboarding and migration are free, but the evaluation step is sales-led rather than self-directed.

### Purpose-Built for Moving - Not a General Tool

Elromco is designed exclusively for moving companies. If you are a general contractor, roofer, plumber, landscaper, or any other trade, Elromco will not fit your operations. The pricing engine is tied to moving rate structures, the dispatch is built for truck-and-crew logistics, the eBOL is a moving bill of lading, and the inventory module is designed for household goods. This is a strength for its target market but a hard boundary.

### Optional Add-Ons Increase the True Cost

While the base pricing is transparent, the optional website builder at $99/mo and extra SMS at $0.025 per message can add to the monthly bill. For a company that needs a new website and sends high SMS volume, the true cost can run $400-500/mo on the Professional plan.

### Lower Independent Review Volume

Outside of Google (512 reviews), Elromco's review presence on Capterra (15 reviews), Software Advice (12 reviews), and GetApp (8 reviews) is smaller than more established moving software platforms. The scores are excellent, but the sample sizes are limited - something to keep in mind when comparing third-party sentiment.
