## Do You Need This Yet?

Glass & glazing software pays for itself when you're doing enough volume that custom orders, lead times, and fragile material handling start creating costly mix-ups. Two quick checks:

- **You don't need it yet** if you're a small shop doing mostly standard installs with predictable inventory. A general field service app or even a paper log still works fine at that scale.
- **You need it now** if you've ever lost a job because glass arrived at the wrong site, or if you're tracking custom orders across multiple suppliers in spreadsheets.

If you're in the middle—growing but not overwhelmed—start with GlazPro. It's built specifically around glazing workflows, so custom order tracking and material scheduling are native features instead of workarounds.
