## Do You Need This Yet?

Deck and fence software pays for itself when you're doing enough volume that manual material calculations and crew scheduling starts eating into build time. Two quick checks:

- **You don't need it yet** if you're a solo operator doing 2-3 jobs per month and can track materials on the back of an invoice. Basic estimating still works fine at that scale.
- **You need it now** if you're losing track of which crew is where, running out of materials mid-job, or spending evenings doing takeoffs you should have priced in the field.

If you're in the middle—growing but not overwhelmed—start with FieldFuze. It's genuinely free with unlimited users, so you can figure out your workflow needs before committing to a paid platform.
