## How to Choose

<p><strong>Start with your estimating complexity.</strong> Do you bid jobs with multiple bond patterns, specialized mortars, or complex layouts? Masonry-specific software handles these calculations properly. Simple residential work might not need trade-specific features.</p>

<p><strong>Consider your full workflow.</strong> Pure estimating tools help you win bids but don't manage projects. All-in-one platforms cost more but handle scheduling, invoicing, and job tracking too.</p>

<p><strong>Factor in your crew size.</strong> Platforms with fixed-fee tiers can make sense for multiple crews, but Contractor Foreman only removes the internal user cap on its Unlimited tier. Per-user pricing gets expensive fast, so compare user limits before buying.</p>
