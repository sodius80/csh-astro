<p>Buildertrend versus JobNimbus. For roofing contractors, this is not a generic construction software debate. It is a choice between a heavy construction platform that happens to do roofing, and a roofing CRM that happens to do some construction.</p>

<p><strong>Disclosure:</strong> Neither vendor currently offers an affiliate program. This comparison is based on verified pricing, feature analysis, and contractor feedback.</p>

<p><strong>Short verdict:</strong> Buildertrend is better for roofing companies with 10+ crews that need full financial tracking and multi-trade support. JobNimbus makes sense when your priority is roofing-specific sales workflows, aerial measurements, and a mobile app your field team will adopt quickly.</p>

## Key decision factors

<p><strong>Do you need full job costing or just pipeline tracking?</strong></p>
<p>Buildertrend includes advanced job costing, change orders, and financial reporting that ties into your accounting. JobNimbus tracks job status and profitability at a higher level. If you have an accountant who wants detailed Work in Progress reports, Buildertrend delivers. If you just need to know which jobs are profitable and which are bleeding, JobNimbus is sufficient.</p>

<p><strong>How important are aerial measurements to your estimating?</strong></p>
<p>JobNimbus integrates directly with EagleView and SkyMeasure, letting you order roof measurements from within the platform. Buildertrend handles measurements through broader construction workflows but lacks the same roofing-native integration. For roofers who estimate multiple jobs daily, this saves real time.</p>

<p><strong>What does your field team need from a mobile app?</strong></p>
<p>JobNimbus built its mobile app specifically for roofing crews. Photo documentation, material orders, and status updates are front and center. Buildertrend's mobile app is capable but carries the weight of its full construction feature set. Techs who only need roofing workflows may find Buildertrend's app busier than necessary.</p>

## Where Buildertrend wins

<p><strong>Financial depth</strong></p>
<p>Buildertrend's job costing and financial reporting go deeper than JobNimbus. If you need to track labor burdens, material markups, and overhead allocation by job, Buildertrend has the ledger-style detail. JobNimbus shows gross profit per job but lacks the same granular cost breakdown.</p>

<p><strong>Multi-trade scalability</strong></p>
<p>If your company does roofing plus siding, gutters, or general remodeling, Buildertrend supports those workflows natively. JobNimbus is built around roofing and can feel limiting when you branch into unrelated trades.</p>

<p><strong>Pre-construction to closeout</strong></p>
<p>Buildertrend handles the full construction lifecycle: lead tracking, estimating, selections, scheduling, job costing, and warranty management. JobNimbus covers sales through production well but thins out on post-production warranty and long-term client management.</p>

<p><strong>Change order control</strong></p>
<p>Roofing jobs rarely go exactly as planned. Storm damage reveals decking issues, code requirements change, and customers upgrade shingle selections mid-job. Buildertrend's change order workflow tracks every revision, updates the job budget automatically, and requires customer approval before work proceeds. JobNimbus can document changes but does not enforce the same financial controls.</p>

## Where JobNimbus wins

<p><strong>Roofing-native workflow</strong></p>
<p>JobNimbus was built for roofers. The terminology, workflows, and integrations reflect that. Statuses like "Approved," "Production Scheduled," and "Completed" map to how roofing companies actually work. Buildertrend uses broader construction language that requires more customization.</p>

<p><strong>Sales team adoption</strong></p>
<p>Roofing sales reps tend to pick up JobNimbus faster. The CRM is built around the roofing sales cycle: lead, inspection, estimate, contract, production. Buildertrend requires more setup and training before sales teams feel productive.</p>

<p><strong>Aerial measurement integration</strong></p>
<p>Ordering EagleView or SkyMeasure reports directly inside your CRM saves an estimated 5-10 minutes per estimate. For high-volume roofing sales operations, that adds up to hours per week. Buildertrend can integrate with measurement services but not as directly.</p>

<p><strong>Photo documentation</strong></p>
<p>JobNimbus mobile app makes photo capture a core feature, not an afterthought. GPS-tagged, timestamped photos attach directly to job records and are critical for insurance claim support. Buildertrend handles photos but within a broader construction context that feels less natural for roofing documentation.</p>

## Pricing reality check

<p>Buildertrend pricing starts at $299/mo for the Standard plan but most roofing companies need Pro at $499/mo or Premium at $900+/mo to get the features that justify the platform. Year one including onboarding typically runs $3,788-$6,888.</p>

<p>JobNimbus pricing is quote-based and varies by user count and feature modules. Public reports suggest Essentials starts around $99/mo, Pro around $199/mo, and Premium around $299/mo for base functionality. The lack of public pricing is frustrating but not unusual in roofing software.</p>

<p>The real cost difference is not just monthly subscription. Buildertrend typically requires 2-4 weeks of onboarding and training. JobNimbus can be operational in 1-2 weeks. For a busy roofing company, that setup time difference translates to real money.</p>

## Dealbreakers

<p><strong>Choose Buildertrend unless:</strong> You are a roofing-only shop with under 10 crews. The platform's complexity and price are hard to justify when JobNimbus handles your core workflow at lower cost and faster setup.</p>

<p><strong>Choose JobNimbus unless:</strong> You need construction-grade financials, manage multiple trades, or require advanced change order tracking. At that point, JobNimbus' simpler reporting becomes a limitation.</p>

## Final verdict

<p><strong>Our call:</strong> Choose Buildertrend if you operate at scale across multiple trades and need construction-grade financial controls. Choose JobNimbus if you are a roofing specialist who wants a CRM that matches your workflow without paying for features you will never use.</p>

<p><strong>Choose Buildertrend if:</strong> You have 10+ crews, need full job costing, manage multiple trades, and have the budget for a premium platform with a longer onboarding curve.</p>

<p><strong>Choose JobNimbus if:</strong> You are a roofing-focused contractor with 3-20 crews, want fast sales team adoption, rely on aerial measurements, and prefer a tool built specifically for your trade.</p>
