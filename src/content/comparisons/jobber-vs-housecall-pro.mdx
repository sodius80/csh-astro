## Where Jobber wins

### 💰 Pricing — Cheaper at every tier

<p>Jobber's Core plan at $49/mo undercuts Housecall Pro's Basic at $65/mo. For a solo operator, that's $192/year saved for comparable functionality.</p>

### 📋 Quoting — More flexible estimates

<p>Jobber's quote builder supports optional line items, product photos, and client-facing online approval. Contractors who win jobs on professional-looking proposals will appreciate this.</p>

### 🔗 Integrations — Broader ecosystem

<p>Jobber connects to more third-party tools out of the box, including deeper QuickBooks sync. If you're piecing together a tech stack, Jobber plays nicer with others.</p>

## Where Housecall Pro wins

### 📱 Mobile app — The best field app in this category

<p>Housecall Pro's iOS and Android apps consistently get higher ratings than Jobber's. Techs in the field report it's faster, more intuitive, and handles offline mode better. If your crew lives in the app, this matters.</p>

### ⭐ Review generation — Built-in Google review requests

<p>Housecall Pro sends automated review request texts after job completion on most plans. Jobber charges extra for this feature. For businesses focused on building their online reputation, this is a real advantage.</p>

## Choose Jobber if…

<ul>
<li>You run 2-8 field technicians</li>
<li>Scheduling & dispatching is your biggest pain point</li>
<li>You want a professional client-facing portal</li>
<li>You need a top-rated mobile app for techs in the field</li>
<li>You're planning to grow in the next 12 months</li>
</ul>

## Choose Housecall Pro if…

<ul>
<li>You're solo or 1-2 techs and budget is tight</li>
<li>You want the absolute fastest setup time</li>
<li>You do a lot of repeat residential customers</li>
<li>GPS tracking of techs isn't critical for you</li>
<li>You want built-in financing options for customers</li>
</ul>

## Final verdict

<p>Our call: <strong>Jobber for most. Housecall Pro if your crew lives in the app.</strong></p>

<p><strong>Choose Jobber if:</strong> You want more features per dollar, better quoting tools, or run a small office where desktop use is common. Also the pick if you're integrating with other software.</p>

<p><strong>Choose Housecall Pro if:</strong> Your techs are in the field all day and the mobile experience is the top priority. Also better if you want automated review generation without paying extra.</p>
